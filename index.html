New York City

Explore New York City

New York City is made up of five boroughs: Queens, Manhattan, Brooklyn, the Bronx, and Staten Island. The city is home to approximately 8 million people. In 1876, France gifted New York City the Statue of Liberty, which is now located on Liberty Island and is a popular tourist destination. Another must-visit destination is Times Square, famous for its towering buildings, Broadway shows, and bright neon signs. This iconic spot was renamed after The New York Times moved its headquarters there. Before that, it was called Longacre Square. New York City is also renowned for its bridges, which connect the boroughs and facilitate transportation.
